Rebuilding Paradise

The World of Dragonbones is full of endless mystery and opportunity. It can be yours to explore, simply follow our guide to the world page. The world is a dangerous place, so stick close and listen carefully.

The Kingdom of Andamaar

Andamaar is located in a beautiful countryside in the foothills of the Frostpeaks. The people are strong, but peaceful and friendly. The Knights of Andamaar are the most powerful fighting force in the world, and a symbol for the unity between the kingdoms.

The Warlords of the Wastes

The Wastes are an inhospitable place, with warring tribes and deadly beasts prowling the badlands. It takes a good guide and some great luck even to pass nearby unharmed.

The Mountain Clans

When the wars raged, some fought with honour and others sought refuge. The Dunduin made the Frostpeaks their home and refuge until the wars were over.

The Merchant Nomads

Ruled by the Great Princes of the Sands, the Merchant Nomads have travelled from Sea to Edge since time immortal. They are ambitious and greedy, yet shrewd, cunning, and brutal. Carriage Hall is their capital, ever on the move and impossible to track unless you are amongst a privileged few.

Warrior

These hardened soldiers have seen it all. From Sea to Edge, they have honed their skills and strength and have the scars to prove it. Warriors can be noble knights, humble peasants, or even cruel raiders. If you have a tendency towards combat, this is the class for you.

Hunter

These cunning outdoorsmen roam the lands, bringing down game for their families or their king. No one knows the land like the hunters, and no one knows how to move unseen like they do. Trackers, poachers, assassins and spies are all over the kingdoms.

Scholar

Those who pick up a book instead of a sword are seldom destined for glory. These are the people who make sure that the world is worth fighting for. Healers, scribes, scientists, and explorers all contribute to the advancement of humanity.

Merchant

With the wars over, it is hgih time to make a quick buck. Merchants from all over flock to Dragonbones to increase their wealth and stake their claim in the exploding peacetime economy.

The World of Dragonbones will be built by those who want to inhabit it. Subscribe to receive updates on our progress.
