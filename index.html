About

The Kingdom of Andar was a beacon of light and hope in the world for hundreds of years. The roads of Andar connected towns and cities from the northern kingdoms to the wildlands in the south. Safety, prosperity, and wealth were abundant for all, and many heroes were made throughout its' grand history.

Years ago, the dark mists descended on the Kingdom of Andar. Dark shapes swirled through them, and the elders whispered that they were sentient. Any who entered the mists were lost forever, and the kingdom began to unravel. Contact between towns just miles apart was severed, and each town was forced to survive on its' own.

For the first time in a generation, the mists have started to recede. The uncovered world is not the same as it was before. Strange, distorted creatures and malevolent entities inhabit the new world. Villages that were close by have vanished and entire cities lost.

Kingdom of Andar

Andar is located in a beautiful countryside in the foothills of the Frostpeaks. The people are strong, peaceful and hard-working. The Knights of Andar are the most powerful fighting force in the world, and a symbol for the unity between the kingdoms.

Marauders

The Marauders are disillusioned soldiers and citizens who sought to profit off of the trusting nature of others. They are made up of slavers, thieves, murderers, and pirates. They reave from the Wastes where they contest the badlands with warring tribes and deadly beasts.

Undead Empire

When the mists rose, so did the unliving. Any who are slain must be burned before dark or they rise again. Far from being mindless, they seem to move as an organized force. They have congregated in the rubble of the ancient city Ruun, the former capital of the long lost Runin Empire.

Beastmen

Powerful, cunning, brutal, and wild, the Beastmen protect the Wildlands fiercely. They have a matriarchal society that honours beasts and nature, and it is rumored that many of them are skinchangers themselves. They hunt demons and the undead and have been seen transporting large quantities of stone and sky metal, but no one knows for what purpose.

Leadership Classes

When the mists descended, many took up the call and defended their homes. Some heroes took up a sword, others a pen.

Warrior

These hardened soldiers have seen it all. They have honed their skills and strength and have the scars to prove it. Warriors can be noble knights, humble peasants, or even cruel raiders. If you have a tendency towards hand to hand combat, this is the class for you.

Hunter

These cunning outdoorsmen roam the lands, bringing down game for their families or their king. No one knows the land like the hunters, and no one knows how to handle a bow like they do. Trackers, poachers, archers, assassins and spies are all over the kingdoms.

Scholar

Those who pick up a book instead of a sword are seldom destined for glory. These are the people who make sure that the world is worth fighting for. Healers, scribes, scientists, and explorers all contribute to the advancement of humanity.

Lord

Whether through politics, birthright, or cunning, these leaders of men have made it to the top. When they speak, others listen. Where they go, others follow. Not the best with a blade, but why need to be when you have a retinue waiting to carry out your every whim.
