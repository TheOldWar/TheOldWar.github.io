The Kingdom of Andamaar

People are our strength.

Pledge your Blade

A land forged in flames.

From the ashes of the War, Andamaar emerged victorious through the strength and guidance of our great leader, High Lord Varen Kalandrius II. After striking the final blow, he forged the Great Pact with all nations. Years later, the Pact is strong and Andamaar has become the center of the world. All roads lead to Kyr, the Capital, and High Lord Varen continues to lead with wisdom and humanity.

Frostpeak Foothills

Andamaar is a beautiful countryside in the foothills north of the Frostpeaks. The land is rich and lush during the summers, but the winters are bitter cold. Trade and farming make Andamaar a wealthy nation, and their Knights are feared and respected throughout the lands.

New Kyr, Capital City

It is said that the City of Kyr is the center of the world and all roads lead to Kyr eventually. Nestled deep in the Frostpeaks, it was founded on an Ancient Runin City believed to be the capital of the Empire. It is surrounded by fertile farmland and is strategically located in a valley near the Khandrys River. Despite surviving numerous sieges, Kyr has never fallen, and many believe it never will.

Artanac

When the wars were in full swing, many from Kyr fled to the relative safety of Artanac. It is located on the edge of the Frostpeaks and Dunduin land. It is well protected by the hostile mountain ranges, and commands a view miles around over the foothills.

Old Harbour

The Gateway to the World, Old Harbour sits on the Lake of Endless Rivers. Dozens of rivers are fed by the lake, making it the trading hub of Andamaar. People from all nations reside there to sell their wares or set out into the world.
